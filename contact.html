Frequently Asked Questions

You can book an appointment by calling us at (555) 123-4567, using our online booking system, or by visiting our clinic in person. For non-urgent appointments, we recommend using our online booking system for the most convenient experience.

We accept most major insurance plans, including Aetna, Blue Cross Blue Shield, Cigna, UnitedHealthcare, and Medicare. Please contact our billing department at (555) 123-4567 ext. 2 to verify if we accept your specific insurance plan.

For your first appointment, please bring your photo ID, insurance card, list of current medications, medical records if available, and any relevant test results or imaging studies. Also, arrive 15 minutes early to complete any necessary paperwork.

Yes, we offer telehealth services for many types of appointments. You can schedule a virtual visit through our online booking system or by calling our office. A stable internet connection and a device with a camera and microphone are required.

We require at least 24 hours' notice for appointment cancellations or rescheduling. A fee may be charged for missed appointments or late cancellations. Please call us as soon as possible if you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment.

Medical Emergency?

If you are experiencing a medical emergency, please call 911 or go to the nearest emergency room immediately.
